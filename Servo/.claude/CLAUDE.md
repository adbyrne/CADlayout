CAD project for Train Order signal servo brackets.

## Key Design Decisions

- **4 mm Y offset on B2**: Fixes arm interference. B1 shelf Y=-9 to -5, B2 shelf Y=-5 to -1.
- **Servo tangent to inner leg**: Servo centre X = inner_leg_edge ± SERVO_R (5.95 mm).
  Maximises servo pocket clearance from bracket wall with zero gap.
- **BodyBoardBehind board positions**: X = ±28 mm, Y = -18 and -37 (past bracket front faces,
  clear of bracket bodies). Base 80 × 47 mm.
- **Fillets applied before fuse**: Corner fillets on shelf/legs applied before fusing to avoid
  OCCT lost-edge problem on outer corners after fusion.
